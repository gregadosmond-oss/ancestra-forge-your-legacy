What's the overall page feel?

Three directions — same sections, very different energy

A

Emotional Narrative

Each section reads like a chapter. Big cinematic headlines. "Your name has been carried for 900 years. It's time you knew the story." Less features, more feeling. Closest to the brand voice — warm, proud, personal.

"Discover your coat of arms. Forge your legacy. Pass it on to the people who matter most."
B

Feature-Forward

Clean, scannable. Icon + headline + one-line description per feature. "Here's exactly what you get." Works well on mobile, easy to build. Feels more like a SaaS product page — clear but less emotionally charged.

"✦ Custom Coat of Arms   ✦ AI Family Story   ✦ Bloodline Tree   ✦ Legacy Certificate"
C

Hybrid — Story + Clarity ★ Recommended

Narrative headlines and emotional copy at the top of each section, clean cards and grids below. You get the emotional pull AND the "what do I get" clarity. Best conversion for a product that needs both heart and specifics.

"Your name has been carried for centuries." → then 4 product cards with clear details
